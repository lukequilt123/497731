Book with us · Six ways to start

Book an appointment — the way that works for you.

Start online, send a message, or pick up the phone. Whichever route you choose, you'll reach the same team of GPs, nurses, and pharmacists ready to help.

01
Fastest · Same-day

RapidHealth — urgent online consultation

Tell us what's wrong through our secure online form. Urgent requests are reviewed the same working day, usually within hours. Best for new symptoms, minor illness, and anything you'd normally call the practice about.

Start request →
02
Non-urgent · Mon–Fri

Patchs — detailed online triage

For longer-term concerns, medication reviews, or questions that don't need a same-day response. Patchs is open Monday to Friday — save time, skip the phone queue.

Open Patchs →
03
Secure messaging

Accurx — messages & video from your GP

Receive secure text messages, questionnaires, and video calls directly from your GP team. If you've been asked to reply or book through Accurx, this is the place.

Visit Accurx →
04
AI-assisted triage

Klinik — smart triage & routing

Klinik uses clinical decision support to route your request to the most appropriate team member — a GP, nurse, pharmacist, or physiotherapist. Answer a few questions and we'll take it from there.

Open Klinik →
05
TPP SystmOne practices

SystmConnect — online services

For practices using TPP SystmOne, SystmConnect gives you direct access to bookings, records, and secure messaging with your practice team.

Open SystmConnect →
06
Full patient engagement

Engage — health management platform

A comprehensive engagement platform linking you with your care team — messages, appointments, health reminders and self-management tools, all in one place.

Open Engage →
111

Urgent but not life-threatening

Call NHS 111 when you need medical help fast but it's not an emergency, or when your practice is closed. Free · 24 hours · 365 days.

999

Life-threatening emergency

Call 999 for chest pain, severe breathlessness, heavy bleeding, stroke symptoms or loss of consciousness — when every second matters.
